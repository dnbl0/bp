# Photography — Retouching photography

*Bupa Brand Guidelines, Version 1 (2023) — Photography section*

By adjusting the crop, light and colour within an image we can achieve a look that is more unique for Bupa.

## How we will improve this image

1. Sharpen image and tighten crop to create more impact
2. Adjust Levels to balance the colour and make less red – care will need to be taken to avoid the skin tones appearing cold
3. Adjust Brightness/Contrast to lighten the faces and create natural lighting
4. Add a touch of blue

## Steps

- **Original**
- **Step 1** — Sharpen image, tighten crop.
- **Step 2** — Adjust Levels to balance colours.
- **Step 3** — Adjust Brightness/Contrast to lighten faces.
- **Step 4** — Add touch of blue.
