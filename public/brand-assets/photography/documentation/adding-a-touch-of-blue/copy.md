# Photography — Adding a touch of blue

*Bupa Brand Guidelines, Version 1 (2023) — Photography section*

## Adding a touch of blue

All images should have a touch of blue. But keep it natural and not over-exaggerated – it can be a minor part of the image.

### Before

These images have no touch of blue in them.

### After

A touch of blue has been added. The images have then been colour balanced and sharpened.

---

Before modifying photographs make sure you have the correct usage rights. Speak to your local brand and legal teams for guidance.
