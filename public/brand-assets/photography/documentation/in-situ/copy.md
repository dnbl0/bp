# Photography — In situ (text over a photo)

*Bupa Brand Guidelines, Version 1 (2023) — Photography section*

## Homepage and header photos

- Select photos where the focal point is top right of the page as this works best on responsive formats
- Choose images that scale down for mobile
- Users pay more attention to relevant images than those that are purely decorative

## Text over a photo

- If text is used over the image it should be limited to a short heading
- The contrast between the image and the text should guarantee the legibility of the text. Adjust the photo if necessary
- Position the message in the part of the image with the least visual noise to boost text legibility
- If the text isn't clear enough on the photo, use a message box

## Please don't

- Cover the focal point of the photo with icons, text or a message box
- Don't use cut out, monotone or black and white imagery

---

Examples shown: image focal area, image focal area in responsive views (desktop and mobile), and text over an image (headline and buttons over a clear low-contrast area; message box with a large amount of text over a busy image).
