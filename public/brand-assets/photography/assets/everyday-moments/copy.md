# Photography — 1. Everyday moments

*Bupa Brand Guidelines, Version 1 (2023) — Photography section*

## Photography overview

Photography is always our first choice over illustration.

Only use the highest quality images. Our imagery captures moments that are observational and feel real, warm and inviting.

### Our four types of photography

1. Everyday moments
2. Bupa delivering care
3. Portraits
4. Everyday still-life

### Let's use images that are

- Authentic, natural and unstaged
- Observations in the moment
- Tighter crops to get closer to people and the moment
- Always have a touch of blue
- Real people with real expressions, not posed
- Highest quality

## 1. Everyday moments

- Everyday people living everyday lives
- Real, authentic people
- Natural expressions
